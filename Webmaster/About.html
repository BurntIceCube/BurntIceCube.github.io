About


Electric cars are better for people to use than normal cars.

By choosing to drive an electric vehicle it releases less pollution to the environment which, would normally come from exhaust emissions of a gasoline powered vehicles.

An electric car is cheaper for it to run since you use electricity to charge which is about one-thirds cheaper than buying gasoline for the same car.

You can use renewable energy to recharge your car, it can reduce your gas emissions. Since it also has less moving parts it will be cheaper to maintain.

There are usually very little need for servicing, no expensive exhaust systems, fuel injection systems, radiators, starter motors, and more.

These cars can help the environment, your health, and to save more money.
